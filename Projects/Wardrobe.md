# Wardrobe

This wardrobe is inspired by the [Hoyez's Wardrobe](https://www.lairdubois.fr/creations/16546-meuble-penderie.html)

Wood
* Fir Wood
* Valchromat: standard MDF stained in the mass for the tray
* Plywood for the drawer


Tools
* Table saw,
* Handled power saw
* Sanders


Joints
* Dowel
